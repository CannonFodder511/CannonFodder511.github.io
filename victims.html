The Victims

Christopher Byers

Christopher Byers

Cause of Death: Multiple injuries.
Manner of Death: Homicide.
Mechanism of Death: Terminal Aspiration*.
Toxicology shows Carbamazepine was found in his system. This is not unexpected as Byers was prescribed Ritalin for ADHD.
Head injuries: Abrasions were present on the right ear, frenulum, nose bridge, both eyebrows, and lips. There were superficial bite marks on both cheeks. There were multiple fractures on the base of the skull. There were abrasions on the neck but no fractures or hemorrhages.
Anal and Genital Injuries: The anus was dilated and there was inflammation in the rectal and anal mucosa. The skin of the penis, scrotal sack and testes were missing. There was a yellow contusion with skin slippage and a gaping wound cause by multiple puncturing and gouging injuries. The left buttocks had 5 superficial cutting wounds. The right buttocks had multiple linear interrupted cuts. There were abrasions present on the anus.
Internal Examination: There were no abnormal fluids in the internal cavities. There was no evidence of blunt force or puncture trauma to the internal organs. There was some vomit in the upper and lower airways, but no water.
No semen was found orally or rectally.
