Timeline

May 5th, 1993

  • Christopher Byers, Steve Branch, and James Michael Moore are reported missing. They are last seen when Branchâ€™s stepfather calls the boys in for dinner. Neighbors and police perform a rudementary search that night.
  • A distraught "Mr. Bojangles" is seen entering the ladies room of a Bojangles a mile from the crime scene. He left blood on the walls.

May 6th, 1993

  • The three boys are found at approximatly 1:45 by police.

May 10th, 1993

  • The autopsy reports of all three victims are released to the police.
  • Chris Morgan and Brian Holland abruptly leave for California.

May 17th, 1993

  • Chris Morgan and Brian Holland are arrested in California. They both take polygraphs are are removed from the list of suspects.

June 3, 1993

  • Police arrest Damien Echols, Jessie Misskelley Jr., and Jason Baldwin and charge them each with three counts of first degree murder.

February 5, 1994

  • A jury convits Misskelley of one count of first degree murder and two counts of second degree murder. He is sentenced to 40 years in prison.

March 18, 1994

  • A jury convits Echols and Baldwin of three counts of capital murder. Baldwin is sentenced to life in prison and Echols is sentenced to death.

August 19, 2011

  • After a successful series of appeals. Echols, Misskelley, and Baldwin are released on time served, but not found innocent.

2021

  • In 2021 a freedom of information act request showed the DNA evidence previously thought to be lost or destroyed still exists. Echols claims his lawyer has since seen the evidence to exist and they are working to petition the Arkansas government to test it.
